FAQ

Common questions

Does Serenia prevent Render / Railway apps from sleeping?

Yes. By pinging your app URL every 3 minutes, Serenia keeps the server warm and prevents free-tier sleep that many hosting platforms enforce after periods of inactivity.

What counts as "Online"?

Any HTTP response with a status code between 200 and 399 is considered online. Timeouts or connection failures mark the site as offline.

How do I export my history?

Click on any monitored website to open the detail view, then hit the "Export CSV" button. You'll receive a downloadable spreadsheet with every check timestamp, status, and response time.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. URLs and monitoring data are stored only in your own database instance. Serenia does not share data with third parties.

Can I monitor APIs, not just websites?

Yes — any publicly accessible HTTP/HTTPS endpoint works, including REST APIs, health-check routes, and webhooks.
